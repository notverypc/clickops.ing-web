Small improvements that make your text easier to scan.

- Use generous line spacing, clear headings, and contrast between text and background.
- Break content into short paragraphs and use lists or blockquotes for emphasis.
- A simple color palette and consistent spacing make your blog feel inviting and polished.

> Good typography helps ideas come through more clearly.
